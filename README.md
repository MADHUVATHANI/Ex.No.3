# Prompt Types with Examples

## 1. Straightforward Prompts

Straightforward prompts are simple and direct instructions given to the AI model. They clearly state what information is required without additional context.

**Example Prompts:**

Prompt 1
Define Artificial Intelligence in healthcare in one sentence.

Response
Artificial Intelligence in healthcare refers to the use of machine learning algorithms and data analysis techniques to assist in medical diagnosis, treatment planning, and healthcare management.

Prompt 2
List three advantages of Artificial Intelligence in healthcare.

Response

1. Improved accuracy in disease diagnosis
2. Faster analysis of medical data
3. Better patient care and treatment planning

---

# 2. Tabular Format Prompting

Tabular prompting asks the AI to present information in a **table format**. This method helps organize data clearly and makes comparisons easier.

**Example Prompt:**

Compare different AI applications in healthcare in a table.

**Response:**

| AI Application       | Description                                   | Example Use Case           |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Medical Imaging      | AI analyzes medical images to detect diseases | Detecting tumors in X-rays |
| Predictive Analytics | AI predicts future health risks using data    | Predicting heart disease   |
| Virtual Assistants   | AI-powered chatbots assist patients           | Symptom checking apps      |
| Drug Discovery       | AI helps discover new medicines               | Faster vaccine development |
| Robotic Surgery      | AI-assisted robots support surgeons           | Minimally invasive surgery |

---

# 3. Preceding Question Prompting

Preceding Question Prompting includes a guiding question before asking the main question. This helps the AI understand the context better and produce a more detailed answer.

**Example Prompt:**

Why is Artificial Intelligence important in healthcare? Explain how AI helps doctors improve diagnosis accuracy.

**Response:**

Artificial Intelligence is important in healthcare because it helps analyze large amounts of medical data quickly and accurately. AI systems can detect patterns in medical images, patient records, and diagnostic tests that may not be easily noticed by humans. By assisting doctors in analyzing medical data, AI improves diagnostic accuracy, reduces human errors, and helps in early detection of diseases such as cancer and heart conditions.

---

# 4. Missing Word Prompting

Missing Word Prompting provides incomplete sentences where the AI fills in the missing word or phrase.

**Example Prompts:**

Prompt 1
Artificial Intelligence systems learn patterns from large amounts of ____.

Response
Artificial Intelligence systems learn patterns from large amounts of **data**.

Prompt 2
In medical imaging, AI helps doctors detect ____ at early stages.

Response
In medical imaging, AI helps doctors detect **diseases** at early stages.

# Observation

* Preceding question prompts encourage **detailed explanations**.
* Missing word prompts test the AI’s ability to **predict and complete sentences**.

These techniques help improve interaction with AI models and make responses more useful depending on the context.
